SAFETY IS OUR TOP PRIORITY

At Indiana Harbor Belt Railroad, we prioritize safety and treat all safety matters with the utmost seriousness. If you have any safety concerns, please contact our Safety Department immediately.

To report a safety concern by phone, contact the Safety Department at (219) 989-4956.


All telephone calls received on the Safety Hotline answering machine will be monitored and logged.

To report a safety concern by email, contact the Safety Department at safety@ihbrr.com.


All emails received in the Safety Department inbox will be monitored and logged.
