WHAT DO WE DO?

We are a Class III railroad and the largest switch carrier in the United States, operating a comprehensive network of 320 miles (510 km) of track. We proudly serve 160 industries, transporting a diverse range of products, including steel, petroleum, chemicals, manufactured goods, and food products.

MORE ABOUT US

WHERE ARE WE LOCATED?

Our main office is located at 2721 161st St, Hammond, IN. Our Blue Island yard in Riverdale, IL, is our largest, while the Gibson Yard in Hammond is among the largest automobile traffic switching operations in the United States. Additional key yards in our network include Burnham, Calumet City, Alsip, Argo, LaGrange, Norpaul, and Michigan Avenue.
