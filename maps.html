EXPLORE IHB'S NETWORK: YARDS AND MAPS FROM O'HARE TO INDIANA

The IHB encircles Chicago from near O'Hare to Northwest Indiana, generally following I-294 and I-80/94. Key yards include Blue Island in Riverdale, IL (a Class 44 track hump yard), Gibson in Hammond, IN (among the largest automobile traffic switching operations), and Michigan Avenue in East Chicago, IN.
